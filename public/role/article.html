Structure

The article role

Description

A section of a page that forms an independent part of a document, page, or site.

Allowed Content

Flow content but with no main element descendants.

Content Category
  • Flow Content
  • Sectioning Content
  • Palpable Content
HTML Elements with Implicit ARIA Role
  • <article>
Allowed HTML Elements
  • <article> (role attribute unnecessary)
  • <div>, <span>, <p>, other elements that can receive any role
Specification Links
